PLACEHOLDER ICONS NEEDED
========================

Please create three icon files in this directory:
- icon16.png (16x16 pixels)
- icon48.png (48x48 pixels)  
- icon128.png (128x128 pixels)

You can:
1. Use an online icon generator (e.g., favicon.io)
2. Design in Figma/Canva and export
3. Use a simple tool like Icon Slate (Mac) or GIMP

Recommended design:
- Purple/blue gradient background (#667eea to #764ba2)
- White circle in center
- Letter "P" or document icon in the center
- Or simply use a solid color background with "P" letter

For quick testing, you can also use simple single-color squares.
